## Featured Projects

### [TrustGateAI](https://github.com/stmarkadebayo/TrustGateAI)
Open-source anti-corruption toolkit with audit-pack analysis, document verification, OCR-assisted checks, registry validation, and grounded compliance chat.

### [TheLawSays](https://github.com/stmarkadebayo/TheLawSays)
AI legal assistant for Nigerian statutes with citation-backed answers, Cloudflare-native retrieval, and web plus mobile clients.

### [Consilium](https://github.com/stmarkadebayo/Consilium)
Multi-persona AI council for decision support, prompt testing, custom advisors, and saved consultation flows.

### [KlavoraAI](https://github.com/stmarkadebayo/KlavoraAI)
Extraction-first document intelligence stack for contracts and policies with fine-tuning, evaluation runbooks, and reproducible training paths.

### [Efiko](https://github.com/stmarkadebayo/Efiko)
AI-powered study companion for document-based learning, contextual Q&A, session memory, and PDF export.

### [Catch That Phish](https://github.com/stmarkadebayo/Catch-That-Phish)
Phishing detection service that monitors emails, classifies messages with ML, and exposes a REST API for integration.

### [VibeCheckr](https://github.com/stmarkadebayo/VibeCheckr)
AI product ideation assistant for validating ideas and generating MVP plans with a tighter build path.

### [Lagos Apartment Pulse](https://github.com/stmarkadebayo/Lagos_Apartment_Pulse)
Real-estate prediction app focused on estimating apartment prices across Lagos from user-selected property features.
